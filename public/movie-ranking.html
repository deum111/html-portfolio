The Best Movies According to Daniel

A few of my top movies of all-time in no particular order.

Elf

The best Christmas movie ever.

Good Will Hunting

Really great drama with great performances from the Robin Williams and Matt Damon.

Interstellar

Awesome visuals directed by Christoper Nolan, one of the best filmmakers of all-time. I also love the iconic soundtrack composed by Hans Zimmer.

Kung Fu Hustle

Hilarious film that should be watched with subtitles. This is unironically the best live-action anime of all-time.

Step Brothers

Wild film with Will Ferrell and John C. Reilly. I love its quotability.

The Goonies

This is a fun movie of a group of misfits going on an adventure. A classic that I enjoyed growing up.

Tropic Thunder

One of the funniest action-comedies of all-time with wild humor and a wild cast of actors.
